    K O N K A G E

    (kon·kah·gey)

    Company Meaning:

    The name KONKAGE is inspired by the anime, Naruto, where the KAGE is the title given to the leader of one of the five great ninja villages.

    In the same way, our company strives to be a leader in our industry through innovative designs and high quality fabrication standards.

    KON is the Chinese Hakka pronunciation of our founder’s family name.

    By combining KON with KAGE, KONKAGE means that regardless of where you work in this company, you are an important member of the family. Because only by us working harmoniously together can we truly become leaders in our industry.

    About the Konkage:

    Hello, my name is The Konkage and I am a designer and craftsman based out of Canada.

    I first fell in love with computers by accidentally downloading this game called Spiral Knights off old Miniclip and became obsessed.

    But as fun as it was, my favourite childhood game had to be the Sims 2. I often planned house layouts on a grid piece of paper before constructing them in the game.

    However, I didn't realise that designing was my passion because school distracted me to think I was better suited in science or commerce.

    So, I went to university for commerce and in the summer before my final year, I stumbled upon this idea for a PC case design. Rather than applying for jobs, I decided to follow my passion and taught myself how to 3D model.

    After graduating, I went to welding school to learn how to fabricate the steel chassis and with the help of a carpenter developed the wood working skills to create the walnut base.

    After countless iterations, I have finally finished a prototype that is nearly market ready and would love your feedback to refine the last few details.

    At the end of the day, it would be my greatest honour to design and craft the PC case that completes your dream set up.
